Weekly Maintenance Detail

Who it's for: High-kilometre drivers, rideshare, premium vehicles, and image-focused business owners.

Inclusions per visit: Exterior wash, wheels/tyres, quick interior vacuum, wipe-down, glass and finish check.

Optional add-ons: Spot stain removal, leather conditioning, spray sealant top-up.

Service area availability: Priority in Newcastle and Lake Macquarie; Hunter Valley and Central Coast by route schedule.

Terms: Vehicle must be in maintainable condition at onboarding. Minimum 8-week commitment. 24-hour cancellation/reschedule window.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start with a one-off detail before joining a plan?

Yes, and it is often the best approach. We can complete an initial reset detail, then shift to weekly, fortnightly or monthly maintenance.

What happens if weather affects my booking?

If weather prevents quality or safe completion, we'll coordinate the next suitable time as early as possible.

Do you provide scheduled mobile detailing in Hunter Valley?

Yes. Scheduled detailing is available across Hunter Valley with route-based booking availability.

Can I combine fleet maintenance with monthly deep-detail rotations?

Yes. Many businesses run frequent maintenance visits with planned deep-detail cycles for selected vehicles.
