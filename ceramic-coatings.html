Frequently Asked Questions

How is ceramic coating scope determined?

Quotes are based on vehicle size, paint condition, correction requirements and selected coating durability level.

What is Kovalent Coatings?

Kovalent Coatings is an Australian-engineered ceramic coating system. As a certified installer, we use their professional-grade products and follow approved application methods for consistent, durable results.

How long does a ceramic coating last?

Depending on the product and aftercare, coatings can last anywhere from 1 to 5+ years. We'll recommend the right option for your use case.

Do I still need to wash my car after a ceramic coating?

Yes, but it's much easier. Dirt and contaminants release more readily, and water sheets off instead of sitting on the surface.
