Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide water or power?

Most jobs can be completed fully mobile with our own setup. If site access limits this, we’ll confirm options before booking.

Can I book regular weekly car detailing in Newcastle?

Yes. Weekly and fortnightly plans are popular across Newcastle for high-use personal and business vehicles.

Is ceramic coating worth it for daily-driven cars?

For many owners, yes. Ceramic coatings help preserve finish quality, reduce maintenance effort and support long-term value.

Do you detail company cars and trade fleets?

Absolutely. Fleet plans are built around operating hours and can include branding-focused presentation standards.

How far in advance should I book?

One to two weeks is recommended in peak periods. Ongoing maintenance clients receive priority scheduling windows.

What payment methods do you accept?

We can confirm current payment options at booking. Commercial fleet clients can request invoice terms by agreement.

Do you offer car detailing across Central Coast suburbs regularly?

Yes, we service Central Coast routes and can include your suburb in recurring run schedules where availability allows.

Can scheduled mobile detailing help maintain resale value?

Regular detailing helps preserve paint, interior condition and presentation, which can support stronger resale outcomes over time.
