Frequently Asked Questions

Is paint correction available in Lake Macquarie suburbs?

Yes. Paint correction services are mobile and available throughout Lake Macquarie with location suitability checks.

What's the difference between single-stage and multi-stage correction?

Single-stage removes light swirls and haze. Multi-stage involves additional cutting and refining passes for deeper defects and maximum clarity.

Should I get paint correction before a ceramic coating?

Yes. Correction removes defects before the coating locks them in. We recommend correcting first for the best possible finish under any coating.
